Web Development
Development on WordPress
The course consists of brief instructional videos where you'll grasp the fundamentals of WordPress. You'll learn how to create websites of varying complexity, from blogs to online stores. Solidify your understanding through practical exercises and test assignments.
Development on Drupal
Unlock the secrets of core and additional Drupal modules (such as Views, Rules, Panels, Devel, Paragraph, and more) as you construct impressive websites on Drupal 8, brick by brick.
